Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2025  |  Jurisdiction: Alberta, Canada  |  Last Updated: April 2025
1. Overview

XAU Signals ("we", "us", "our") operates xausignals.com and all associated products including Agent Levz™, the XAU Security Agent, and the XAU Signals Skool community (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian privacy law.

We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Alberta's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

✓ Our Commitment

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We never have and never will.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information across our products:

Data Collected Why We Collect It Retention
Email address Account creation, license delivery, product updates, support Duration of account + 2 years
Payment information Processing purchases via Stripe. We never see or store your card number — Stripe handles all card data. Transaction records kept 7 years (tax/legal requirement)
Telegram user ID / Chat ID Delivering signal alerts and security agent notifications Duration of active subscription
License key & hardware fingerprint Validating XAU Security Agent licenses and preventing unauthorised sharing Duration of license + 1 year
Usage & analytics data Understanding how features are used to improve the product (aggregated where possible) 13 months rolling
Support communications Resolving support requests 3 years
What We Do NOT Collect
  • Your trading account credentials or exchange API keys
  • The contents of files on your machine
  • Your wallet addresses or crypto balances
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Information about anyone under 18 — our Services are for adults only
3. How We Use Your Information
  • Delivering the Services you purchased — signals, license keys, community access
  • Sending transactional emails — purchase confirmations, license delivery, security alerts
  • Providing customer support when you contact us
  • Improving our products based on aggregated usage patterns
  • Meeting our legal obligations under Canadian tax and business law
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or Terms of Service violations
✓ No Advertising

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or selling to data brokers. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit opt-in.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only with the following service providers, and only to the extent necessary to deliver our Services:

Provider What They Receive Purpose
Stripe Email address, transaction amount Payment processing
Render Email address, license data (encrypted at rest) Hosting license server and backend services
Skool Email address, name Community platform for courses
Telegram Chat ID for alert delivery Signal and security alert notifications

We may also disclose your information where required by law, court order, or government authority in Canada.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your personal information is stored on servers located in the United States (Render) and is subject to US data protection laws in addition to Canadian law. By using our Services, you consent to this cross-border transfer.

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all web traffic
  • Encrypted storage for license and account data on Render
  • Stripe PCI DSS compliance for all payment data — we never store card numbers
  • Access controls limiting who can access personal data
⚠ Security Notice

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security issue involving your XAU Signals account, contact us immediately at xauaisignals@gmail.com.

6. Cookies & Analytics

xausignals.com uses cookies for session management, analytics, and functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or share browsing data with ad networks.

  • Session cookies — keeping you logged in during your visit
  • Analytics — understanding which pages are visited (aggregated, not individual tracking)
  • Functionality — remembering your preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not function correctly without them.

7. Your Rights Under Canadian Privacy Law

Under PIPEDA and Alberta PIPA, you have the following rights:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Withdrawal of consent — Withdraw consent to our use of your information (may affect your ability to use the Services)
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Complaint — File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada or the OIPC Alberta

To exercise any of these rights, email xauaisignals@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

8. Data Retention
  • Active account data — retained for the duration of your account
  • Transaction records — 7 years (Canadian tax law requirement)
  • Support communications — 3 years
  • Analytics data — 13 months rolling
  • Deleted account data — purged within 90 days of deletion request, except where legal retention applies
9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, contact xauaisignals@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify active customers by email if the changes are material.
