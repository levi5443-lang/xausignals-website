Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon use

XAU Signals is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA).

1. Who We Are

XAU Signals operates xauSignals.com and AI Agent Levz. We are based in Alberta, Canada. For privacy inquiries contact us at support@xausignals.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account data Email address, name To deliver your invite code and manage your subscription
Payment data Billing information processed by Stripe To process subscription payments
Telegram data Telegram chat ID and username To deliver signals via @AgentLevz
Usage data Signal delivery logs, command usage To improve service performance
Technical data IP address, browser type (via website) For security and analytics

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, financial account numbers, or health information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver your subscription invite code and signal access
  • Process payments and manage billing via Stripe
  • Send service-related emails including access codes and updates
  • Notify you of subscription renewals and expirations
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of AI Agent Levz signals
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure hosted on AWS. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Payment information is never stored on our servers — all payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe using industry-standard SSL encryption.

Your Telegram chat ID is stored to deliver signals. We do not have access to your Telegram messages or contacts beyond interactions with @AgentLevz.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your subscription is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@xausignals.com.

7. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

As a Canadian resident you have the following rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA):

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to withdrawal — withdraw consent for collection and use of your data (note: this may affect your ability to use the service)
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to complain — file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada at priv.gc.ca

To exercise any of these rights please contact us at support@xausignals.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

xauSignals.com uses minimal cookies for basic website functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar third-party tracking services.

Stripe may set cookies during the payment process for fraud prevention and security purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information please contact us immediately.

10. GDPR — European Union & United Kingdom

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or UK GDPR applies to your personal data. In addition to your rights under PIPEDA you have the following rights:

  • Right to data portability — receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")

Lawful basis for processing: We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to deliver your subscription and signals
  • Legitimate interests — improving our service, fraud prevention, and security
  • Consent — for marketing communications (you may withdraw at any time)
  • Legal obligation — where required by applicable law

International data transfers: Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) including Canada and the United States. These transfers occur when using our third-party services (Stripe, Supabase, Resend, Render). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through standard contractual clauses or reliance on adequacy decisions where applicable.

Data breach notification: In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay.

To exercise your GDPR rights or to lodge a complaint you may also contact your local supervisory authority. EU residents may contact the supervisory authority in their member state. UK residents may contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

11. CCPA — California Residents

If you are a California resident the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the following rights:

  • Right to know — request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to delete — request deletion of personal information we have collected from you
  • Right to opt-out — we do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise your CCPA rights please contact us at support@xausignals.com. We will respond within 45 days.

12. International Users

XAU Signals accepts customers from around the world. By using our service from outside Canada you consent to the transfer of your personal information to Canada where our primary data processing occurs. We apply the same privacy standards to all users regardless of location.

Users in Australia are protected under the Australian Privacy Act 1988. Users in New Zealand are protected under the Privacy Act 2020. Users in Singapore are protected under the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). The rights and principles described in this policy are consistent with these frameworks.

13. Cookies

xauSignals.com uses minimal cookies for basic website functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

We use a cookie consent mechanism to comply with EU/UK ePrivacy requirements. You may accept or decline non-essential cookies when first visiting the site. Essential cookies required for the website to function cannot be disabled.

Stripe sets cookies during the payment process for fraud prevention and security. These are classified as strictly necessary and do not require consent.
